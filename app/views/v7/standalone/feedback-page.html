Submit request to change contact details

You can log in to the NHS website or use the NHS App to change your mobile phone number or email address. Find out how to change your contact details on the NHS website and NHS App.

Otherwise you need to contact your GP surgery.

If you are not registered with a GP surgery or you have moved outside the catchment area of your current surgery, you can find one using our Find a GP service.

Do not include confidential or medical information in your message. For example, your NHS number, date of birth, medical diagnosis or medical history.
