Add or correct your contact details

    In research

  • A citizen is notified (SMS or email) about either a missing or malformed (invalid) contact detail and directed to a service to rectify it.

    They are linked to or find a standalone web service that that takes their details and matches them to their PDS record or allows to sign in with NHS login.

    If not an NHS login user - A verification check happens on their good contact detail. If successful, they can then change the bad contact detail.

    • ➡️ User gets an email  (missing mobile) | (malformed mobile)
    • ➡️ User gets an SMS  (missing email) | (malformed email)
    • ➡️  Service start page | (run in devMode)

    Changes in this prototype

    1. Hybrid notifications (displays both a link and instructions)
    2. Start page has reduced content
    3. Triage step at start of journey
    4. NHS login integration
    5. NHS login account provisioning at end of journey
    6. Usability improvements to the forms
