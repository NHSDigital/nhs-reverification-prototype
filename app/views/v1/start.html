Add or correct your contact details

We use the contact details on your NHS record to get in touch with you about screening, vaccinations or other appointment reminders.

It's important that your contact details are correct, as your GP and other NHS services will use them to contact you.

You can update your email address or phone number by using this service.

This will not update your NHS login details.

Before you start

You will need to have access to your email account and phone.

To verify it's you, we will need to send a security code to the contact details we already have for you.

Once confirmed, we'll send a different security code to the contact details you're adding, to check it's working correctly.
