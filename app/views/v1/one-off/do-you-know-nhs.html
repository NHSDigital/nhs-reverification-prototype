Do you know your NHS number?

Telling us your NHS number will give us the best chance of matching you to your NHS record.

If you do not know it, you can tell us your postcode instead.
How to find your NHS number

An NHS number is a 10 digit number, like 485 777 3456.

You can find this:

  • on any letter or document sent to you by the NHS
  • by logging in to a GP practice online service
  • by using the NHS number finder service (opens in a new window)

Ask your GP surgery for help if you can't find your NHS number.
