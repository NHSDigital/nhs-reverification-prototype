Information:

If you think it's a life threatening emergency:

  • call 999

If you do not think it's an emergency:

  • call 111 or go to 111 online
  • call 111 or speak to a pharmacist, GP or dentist
  • search the NHS website health A-Zfor more advice about your condition

The NHS website team does not access to individual patient information. If you have any questions or concerns about your patient record, please contact your current GP surgery.

Do not include confidential or medical information in your message. For example, your NHS number, date of birth, medical diagnosis or medical history.
