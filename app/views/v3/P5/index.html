Update your contact details

We use the contact details on your NHS record to get in touch with you about screening, vaccinations or other appointment reminders.

It's important that your contact details are correct, as your GP and other NHS services will use them to contact you.

You can update your email address or mobile phone number by using NHS login.

This will not update your NHS login details.

Continue with NHS login

If you do not want to use NHS login, or create an NHS login account, you can update your details by using our one off service.

Before you start

You will need access to your email account and mobile phone.

To verify it's you, we will send a security code to the contact details we already have for you.

Once confirmed, we'll send a different security code to the contact details you're adding, to check it's working correctly.
